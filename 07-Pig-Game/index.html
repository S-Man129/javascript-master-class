How to Play the Pig Game

  1. Roll the Dice 🎲
    • Click "Roll Dice" to roll a number between 1–6.
    • The number adds to your current score.
  2. Be Careful!
    • If you roll a 1, your current score resets to 0, and it's the next player's turn.
  3. Hold to Keep Score
    • Click "Hold" to add your current score to your total score and end your turn.
  4. Win the Game 🏆
    • The first player to reach 100 points wins!
